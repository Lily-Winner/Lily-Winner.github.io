3D Runner Game

×

3D computer game, Runner, developed using the Unity engine. The game environment features low-poly objects crafted in Blender software.

The main objective of the game is to survive, collide, and crush as much food as possible to accumulate points. The player is a flying fork soaring above a long bar table. On this table are various food dishes from world cuisines; however, beware of these tempting delicacies, as colliding with them can deplete the life score. As you progress through the game, the speed dynamically changes, adding an extra layer of challenge and excitement.

One distinctive feature of the game is its exploration of diverse food locations inspired by countries around the world. Currently, only one location is accessible.

2D Survval Game

×

A 2D computer, casual game named 'Microvival' is project centered around bacteria and... colors. The game developed in Unity engine. Sprites combined with hand drawings. Using minimalism in the color of environment and UI design to highlight the main theme - Color! :).

The protagonist is a bacterium, and the objective is to score as many points as possible by consuming other bacteria. However, the unique twist lies in maintaining an equilibrium of color percentages.

At the game's onset, the player starts with a gray color, representing fifty percent of the three primary colors (RGB). As the player devours other bacteria during the game, he absorb color percentages from their prey, altering own hue. To add an extra layer of complexity, if the player refrains from consuming colored bacteria for a certain duration, his color starts to change independently. This dynamic feature not only shortens the gameplay but also compels the player to remain attentive.

Survival in 'Microvival' hinges on skillfully managing color balance and avoiding the black color, which marks the end of the game. The development process of the game revolves around the manipulation of RGB colors, intricate dependencies, and the dynamic alteration of colors based on the player's choices.

Beyond the gaming experience, 'Microvival' serves as an educational tool, fostering an intuitive understanding of colors. It challenges players to feel and comprehend colors on an instinctive level while providing an entertaining way to pass the time.

"Island System" Game

×

3D Third person, quest and jorney game. "Island System" is an exploration and resource management game set in a world of interconnected islands. The game has SFX sounds, weather changes and tooltips. The entire game design: environment, assets, textures and animations, were created in Unreal Engine, using the tols that are in the engine itself.

Player start on a starting island and must navigate through different environments, solve mysteries, and collect resources to progress to the final island. The game incorporates elements of platforming: player construct platforms using collected resources to overcome obstacles and reach new areas. Each island offers unique challenges and resources to uncover.

Island Exploration: Player navigate through a series of islands, each with its own distinct environment, terrain, and resources. The islands are interconnected, and player must find the path to the next island.

Resource Collection: Player collect various resources scattered throughout the islands. Resources are essential for crafting platforms and unlocking new areas. Each resource board has its own properties and characteristics.

Crafting and Platforming: Player use the collected resources to craft platforms and build bridges, allowing them to traverse gaps, reach higher platforms, and overcome obstacles. trategically Crafting platforms is important as resources may be limited. Mystery Solving: Each island presents mysteries and challenges for player to solve.

Randomized Resource Placement: Resources, trees, and other environmental elements appear in random locations within the islands. This ensures that each playthrough offers a fresh experience, requiring player to explore different areas and adapt their strategies. The trees environment is randomized according to the material of the earth, and it’s location.

The ultimate goal: To reach the final island by successfully navigating through all the islands, solving mysteries, a nd collecting sufficient resources to construct platforms and bridges.
